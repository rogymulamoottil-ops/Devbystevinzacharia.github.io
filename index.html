What is Artificial Intelligence?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the simulation of human intelligence in machines that are programmed to think, learn and make decisions.

AI technology is transforming industries such as healthcare, education, transportation, business and robotics.

With AI, computers can recognize speech, understand images, solve problems, and even interact like humans.
